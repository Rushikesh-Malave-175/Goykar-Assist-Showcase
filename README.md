# Goykar-Assist-Showcase
A platform for local  users to avail as well as share their skills  online. It connects the providers of any  service with users seeking that service.

### Features
 1. A platform to PROVIDE and AVAIL services. No matter how big or how small.
 2. Geolocation enabled - gives an estimate of within how much time you’ll get that service to your 
doorstep.
 3. Skill verification and review system: makes it easy for customers to find quality service providers.
 4. Smart search algorithms & bidding system: Suggests customers best service providers based on 
Distance, Price, Ratings, and Availability.
 5. Scheduling & Availability: Allows service providers to set available HOURS/DAYS. Lets users book 
free slots directly.
 6. Payments: Immediate and escrow system (pay post job completion).
 7. Dashboard & Admin panel: Users can see their bookings, history, wallet, ratings/review etc.
 8. Safety and Moderation: Users/Service providers/Inappropriate content can be reported. Admin 
login with control panel.
 9. Offers and rewards: Reward repeating customers with offers/coupons/festival based discounts. 
10. Community & feedback: Customers can give feedback on the service providers. And 
vice-versa as well.
